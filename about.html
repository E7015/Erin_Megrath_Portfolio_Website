About Me

"For me, great design isn't just about solving problems, it's about anticipating needs, reducing friction, and making technology feel more natural."

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with roots in illustration and a strong focus on interaction design. My background in visual storytelling has shaped how I think about aesthetics and narrative, how they influence perception, guide attention, and create meaning.

Curiosity about human behaviour and cognitive psychology drives my design process. I approach every project with empathy, aiming to craft experiences that feel intuitive, purposeful, and human-centred.
